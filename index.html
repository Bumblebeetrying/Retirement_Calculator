Actuarial Methodology Note

Accumulation: Growing annuity formula FV = C₁ × [(1+r)ⁿ - (1+g)ⁿ] / (r-g) rather than flat-contribution FV. Age-banded salary scales (12%/8%/5%) rather than single flat rate. Monthly compounding for NPS (daily NAV); annual crediting for EPF.


Real Returns: Fisher equation: r_real = (1+r_nominal)/(1+π) - 1. Not simple subtraction. At 8.25% EPF and 5.5% CPI: real return = 2.61%, not 2.75%.


Longevity: IALM 2006-08 Indian Assured Lives Mortality tables. Planning to an 80th percentile survival horizon rather than median life expectancy.


Required Corpus: Expenditure at retirement (inflation-adjusted) ÷ Sustainable Withdrawal Rate (2.75%). SWR adjusted from US Trinity Study 4% to reflect India's structurally higher inflation.


WeCare Commutation: Factor of 150× derived from annuity pricing: a_60 = Σ v^k × ₖp₆₀, evaluated at 6.5% discount and IALM mortality. Sensitivity: at 5.5% discount, factor = ~170×; at 7.5%, factor = ~130×.


Tax: EPF = EEE (contributions within 80C, accumulation and withdrawal exempt if service ≥5yr). NPS = EET (60% lump sum exempt; 40% annuity taxable as income). WeCare = partially exempt per Income Tax Act §10(10A).
